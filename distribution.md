# HAJI Distribution Schedule

HAJI is a Solana-based token with a fixed total supply of 10,000,000,000 tokens.

Current distribution approach:
- Liquidity pool allocation: a portion of supply has been added to the HAJI/SOL liquidity pool on Raydium.
- Treasury / reserve: remaining supply is held by project-controlled wallets.
- Future use: tokens may be used for membership access, holder perks, community participation, rewards, and ecosystem development.

HAJI is intended as a community token focused on access, engagement, and future participation. Holders should always verify the official mint address before interacting with any token.
